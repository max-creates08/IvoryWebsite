Ivory Notes help

Support

Straightforward help for recording, transcription, storage, widgets, and Apple Watch.

Ivory Notes cannot access my microphone
  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
  2. Go to Privacy & Security, then Microphone.
  3. Enable access for Ivory Notes.
  4. Return to Ivory Notes and try recording again.
My recording did not produce a transcript

Make sure the recording contains clear human speech and that your device has enough free storage. Keep Ivory Notes open while transcription completes. Very quiet audio, heavy background noise, or unsupported language may produce an incomplete or empty transcript.

Does Ivory Notes require an internet connection?

No. Ivory Notes includes its transcription model and can record, transcribe, organize, and review notes without an internet connection. App Store downloads and Apple system services may still require connectivity.

How do I delete audio without deleting the note?

Open Ivory Notes Settings and find Files by Note. Swipe left on a recording and choose Delete Audio. The transcript and note will remain.

How do I remove everything from Ivory Notes?

Open Ivory Notes Settings, go to Storage, and choose Delete All Notes & Audio. This action cannot be undone, so export or retain anything you need before confirming.

My Apple Watch recording has not appeared on iPhone

Keep the Watch near its paired iPhone, confirm Bluetooth and Wi-Fi are enabled, and open Ivory Notes on the iPhone. Transfers may wait until the devices reconnect. Also confirm that Ivory Notes is installed and available in the Watch app on iPhone.

How do I add Ivory Notes widgets?

On the Home Screen, touch and hold an empty area, tap Edit, then Add Widget and search for Ivory Notes. For Lock Screen widgets, touch and hold the Lock Screen, tap Customize, choose Lock Screen, and select Ivory Notes in the widget area.

How can I get a better recording?

Place the iPhone close to the speaker, avoid covering its microphones, and reduce background noise where possible. AirPods or a compatible external microphone can help in larger rooms.

What should I include in a bug report?

Include your iPhone or iPad model, iOS or iPadOS version, Ivory Notes version from Settings, what you expected, what happened, and the exact steps that reproduce the problem. Attach a screenshot of any error, but remove private note or recording content first.

Still need help?

Email support@useivory.app. Support requests are normally answered in two business days. Never send private recordings or transcripts unless they are necessary to explain your issue and you are comfortable sharing them.
