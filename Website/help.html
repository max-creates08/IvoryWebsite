Ivory Note help

Help

Support, troubleshooting, and privacy information for recording, transcription, storage, widgets, and Apple Watch.

Which languages does Ivory Note support?

Ivory Note 1.3 automatically detects the spoken language during on-device transcription. The app interface is available in English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Simplified Chinese, and Russian.

What happens if a recording is interrupted?

Ivory Note keeps the recording audio safe. If another app or the system interrupts recording, return to Ivory Note and choose Resume Recording or Save Note. If transcription cannot finish, you can still save the recovered audio instead of losing the recording.

Can Ivory Note keep recording when I leave the app?

Yes. An active recording continues in the background when iOS permits it. Return to Ivory Note to stop and process the recording. A phone call, audio route change, or system interruption may pause recording and show the resume-or-save prompt.

How does Ivory Note title a long recording?

Ivory Note analyzes repeated subjects across the complete transcript and favors the dominant topic instead of the greeting or first sentence.

Ivory Note cannot access my microphone
  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
  2. Go to Privacy & Security, then Microphone.
  3. Enable access for Ivory Note.
  4. Return to Ivory Note and try recording again.
My recording did not produce a transcript

Make sure the recording contains clear human speech and that your device has enough free storage. Keep Ivory Note open while transcription completes. Very quiet audio, heavy background noise, or unsupported language may produce an incomplete or empty transcript.

Does Ivory Note require an internet connection?

No. Ivory Note includes its transcription model and can record, transcribe, organize, and review notes without an internet connection. App Store downloads and Apple system services may still require connectivity.

How do I delete audio without deleting the note?

Open Ivory Note Settings and find Files by Note. Swipe left on a recording and choose Delete Audio. The transcript and note will remain.

How do I remove everything from Ivory Note?

Open Ivory Note Settings, go to Storage, and choose Delete All Notes & Audio. This action cannot be undone, so export or retain anything you need before confirming.

My Apple Watch recording has not appeared on iPhone

Keep the Watch near its paired iPhone, confirm Bluetooth and Wi-Fi are enabled, and open Ivory Note on the iPhone. Transfers may wait until the devices reconnect. Also confirm that Ivory Note is installed and available in the Watch app on iPhone.

How do I add Ivory Note widgets?

On the Home Screen, touch and hold an empty area, tap Edit, then Add Widget and search for Ivory Note. For Lock Screen widgets, touch and hold the Lock Screen, tap Customize, choose Lock Screen, and select Ivory Note in the widget area.

How can I get a better recording?

Place the iPhone close to the speaker, avoid covering its microphones, and reduce background noise where possible. AirPods or a compatible external microphone can help in larger rooms.

What should I include in a bug report?

Include your iPhone or iPad model, iOS or iPadOS version, Ivory Note version from Settings, what you expected, what happened, and the exact steps that reproduce the problem. Attach a screenshot of any error, but remove private note or recording content first.

Still need help?

Email support@useivory.app. Support requests are normally answered in two business days. Never send private recordings or transcripts unless they are necessary to explain your issue and you are comfortable sharing them.

Ivory Note legal

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

Ivory Note is designed to keep your voice notes private and under your control.

Overview

Ivory Note is an offline-first voice-notes application developed by MRX Dynamics LLC. Ivory Note does not require an account and does not operate a server that receives your recordings, transcripts, notes, summaries, study guides, flashcards, or other generated note content.

Information Ivory Note handles

To provide its features, Ivory Note handles content that you choose to create or import:

  • Microphone audio recorded through Ivory Note.
  • Audio files you import into the app.
  • Recordings transferred from your paired Apple Watch.
  • Transcripts, titles, summaries, subjects, study guides, flashcards, and other note content generated from your content.
  • App preferences, such as whether onboarding has been completed.

How processing works

Speech transcription runs on your device using WhisperKit and a speech model included with Ivory Note. WhisperKit loads the bundled model from the app package and runs transcription locally using Apple/Core ML infrastructure. Model downloads are disabled in this build.

Ivory Note does not use the OpenAI API and does not send recordings, transcripts, summaries, study guides, flashcards, or other generated note content to OpenAI or any other third-party AI service.

Storage and deletion

Recordings and note data are stored locally in Ivory Note's app container on your device. You can delete an individual recording while retaining its note, delete a complete note, or delete all notes and recordings from Ivory Note's Settings.

Deleting Ivory Note from a device generally removes its local app data from that device. Device backups are controlled by your Apple and device settings, not by Ivory Note.

Apple Watch

The Ivory Note Apple Watch app records audio locally on the Watch and transfers that audio to the paired iPhone using Apple's device communication services. Transcription and note generation occur on the iPhone.

Permissions

Ivory Note requests microphone access only to record audio. You can review or revoke microphone permission in the Settings app on your device. Features that require microphone access will not work when permission is denied.

Data collection, tracking, and advertising

Ivory Note does not require account registration, display third-party advertising, include third-party analytics SDKs, or track you across apps and websites. Ivory Note does not share personal data with a third-party AI service.

Apple may independently process limited information when you download Ivory Note, submit an App Store review, use TestFlight, or use Apple system services. Apple's handling of that information is governed by Apple's own privacy policies.

Children's privacy

Ivory Note does not knowingly collect personal information from children through an Ivory Note-operated service. Ivory Note is not presented as a children's app. Parents and guardians should supervise recording and device use as appropriate.

Recording other people

You are responsible for obtaining permission before recording other people and for following the laws, school rules, workplace policies, and other requirements that apply where you are located. Ivory Note does not determine whether a particular recording is lawful.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when Ivory Note's features or data practices change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when a revised policy is published.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to support@useivory.app.
