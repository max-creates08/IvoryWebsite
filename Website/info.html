Version history

What is included.

Release notes will be updated as new builds ship to the App Store.

1.3
Recording reliability update June 2026

Safer recordings and smarter study tools

  • Continue recording when Ivory Note moves to the background.
  • Resume an interrupted recording or save it as a note.
  • Keep recoverable audio if transcription cannot finish.
  • Identify recordings created on iPad correctly.
  • Create clearer titles, summaries, study guides, and flashcards.
  • Improve language handling, spacing, alignment, and contrast.
1.1
Multilingual update June 2026

More languages and stronger titles

  • Detect the spoken language during on-device transcription.
  • Use Ivory Note in seven interface languages.
  • Generate titles from the full context of long recordings.
1.0
Initial release June 2026

Private voice notes for study

  • Record classes, meetings, ideas, and study sessions.
  • Transcribe on device without an Ivory Note account.
  • Create titles, summaries, study guides, and flashcards.
  • Sort by subject, favorite, source, or newest first.
  • Capture from Apple Watch and transfer back to iPhone.
  • Delete audio while keeping the note text.
